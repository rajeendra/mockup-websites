About the company

Consulting Engineers and Architects Associated (CEA) was established in February, 1979, by Neville Kotagama, a senior Highway Engineer. The aim of this engineering consultancy firm was to undertake multi-disciplinary consultancy work with an emphasis on the Highways and Transport, Water supply and Drainage, Architecture, Urban Planning, Environment Management and Social Science, Water Resources and Irrigation sectors.

The firm was incorporated in 2007 as a Limited Liability Company and CEA offices are located in Sri Jayawardenapura, Kotte, the administrative capital of Sri Lanka. A majority of the shareholders and the management of the company are Engineers.

Our Vision, Mission and Core Values

CEA aims to provide comprehensive consultancy services to all clients integrating the various disciplines necessary to carry out projects from inception to successful completion. The firm places importance to providing an economical service to the clients and lays emphasis on the appropriate design procedures and efficient Contract and Construction Management Services to ensure successful and speedy completion of the projects. We have the necessary expertise at management level to identify and intervene early to take corrective actions in the unlikely event of any potential problems in our assignments.

The firm places great importance to the co-ordination at all stages of project development and Directors and Senior Associates oversee all activities of an assignment from inception to completion. The firm also can provide overall management services for major projects, which include development of master plans and programmes, carrying out feasibility studies, environmental social impact assessments, detailed designs, project management, administration, progress and financial control of contracts and construction management.

We operate under the premise of customer being the king, quality and continuous improvement as the core ingredient of success, respecting and valuing our employees and giving them opportunities to be the best they can be as the foundation of our growth, and innovation as the key to our survival.
